How does the Cash Gift Registry Work?

The Cash Gift Registry is here to provide convenient access to the gift that
matters most, CASH. Weddings, bat and bar mitzvahs, house warmings,
baby showers bridal showers, all of these and any gift giving event is perfect for The Cash Gift Registry.

The Cash Gift Registry's processes are simple.
Registration is completed in five easy steps:

      Fill in information on yourself and your event

      Set up your account

      Confirms your information

      Choose your gifts

      Send electronic invitations to your friends and family


Once the registration is completed you will receive:

        A confirmation email

       Optional notification mailer cards

        One The Cash Gift Registry gift card (Visa/MasterCard imprinted, and ATM accessible)
             for access to their cash gifts


Fees:

         There are no fees charged to registrants.

         There is a minimal service charge (charged to the purchaser)
            for each cash gift purchase.
